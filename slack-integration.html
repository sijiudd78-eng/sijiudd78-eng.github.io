Slack Best Practices & Etiquette

✅ Do's:
  • Be respectful and professional
  • Use appropriate channels for your questions
  • Search before asking common questions
  • Share helpful tips and experiences
  • Use threads for long conversations
  • React with emojis to acknowledge messages
❌ Don'ts:
  • Spam multiple channels with the same message
  • Share personal or sensitive information
  • Use inappropriate language or content
  • Tag multiple people unnecessarily
  • Post off-topic content in support channels
  • Share external links without permission
